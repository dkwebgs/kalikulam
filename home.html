Apparition of the Virgin Mary in KALLIKULAM
FROM, 1939

According to tradition, the Virgin Mary appeared to a young girl in Kallikulam, India, in 1939. The apparition occurred near a small pond where the girl was fetching water. Mary appeared in a brilliant light, dressed in white and blue, and delivered a message of peace and conversion.

The site has since become a place of pilgrimage, with many reported miracles and healings attributed to the intercession of Our Lady of Kallikulam. A small chapel was built at the location, which later expanded into a church that attracts thousands of pilgrims annually.
